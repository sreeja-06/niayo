Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the incorporation process take?

Typically, the complete incorporation process takes 15-20 working days, depending on the type of company and documentation requirements.

What documents are required for incorporation?

Required documents include ID proof, address proof, PAN card, passport photos, and business address proof. We'll provide a complete checklist during consultation.

Do you provide post-incorporation services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-incorporation services including compliance management, tax filing, and business advisory services.

What types of companies can you incorporate?

We can help incorporate Private Limited Companies, Public Limited Companies, One Person Companies (OPC), and Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP).
