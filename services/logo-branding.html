Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a logo design package?

Our logo design packages include multiple concepts, revisions, source files, and brand guidelines. The exact features depend on the package you choose.

How long does the design process take?

Typically, a complete logo and branding project takes 2-3 weeks, depending on the complexity and number of revisions required.

Do I own the rights to my logo?

Yes, once the project is completed and final payment is made, you own full rights to your logo and brand assets.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive your logo in multiple formats including AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, and JPG, suitable for both print and digital use.
